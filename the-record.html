I.US Clinical Trial Contributions
Veteran of pivotal Phase III clinical trials in atopic dermatitis (dupilumab) under Dr. Emma Guttman-Yassky, Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, 2016-2018. These multicenter randomized controlled trials contributed to the FDA approval of Dupixent and transformed the standard of care in moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis.
Veteran of multiple Phase III clinical trials in psoriasis evaluating next-generation biologic therapies under Dr. Mark Lebwohl, Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, 2016-2018. Contributed to the clinical development programs of several biologics that subsequently received FDA approval and redefined treatment paradigms in moderate-to-severe plaque psoriasis.
