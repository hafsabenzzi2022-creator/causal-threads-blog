04 — When to Use What

Quick Rules

Blog post about methodology → Thread Gradient + DAG diagrams
White paper / deep-dive → DAG Constellation + Confound Gold accents
Personal narrative / essay → Editorial Parchment + warm tones
LinkedIn teaser → Dark social card + Thread Red wordmark
Tutorial / how-to → Collider Teal accents + code in JetBrains Mono
Genomics / precision med → Mediator Violet + Counterfactual Blue accents

The One Rule

Every visual should feel like it was made by someone who understands both the beauty of a well-specified DAG and the texture of handmade paper. Science and craft. Precision and warmth. That's the thread.
